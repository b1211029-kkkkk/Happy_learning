字彙測驗（多益 550 分）

1. The meeting was _____ because the manager was sick.

cancelled
cancel
cancelling
cancels

✔ cancelled
解析：被動語態需使用過去分詞。

2. Please _____ the form before Friday.

submit
delay
refuse
ignore

✔ submit
解析：submit 表示提交文件。

3. The company plans to _____ new workers.

hire
fire
retire
borrow

✔ hire
解析：hire 表示雇用。

4. This product is very _____ among young people.

popular
boring
expensive
difficult

✔ popular
解析：popular 表示受歡迎的。

5. He is responsible _____ customer service.

for
to
with
at

✔ for
解析：be responsible for 為固定用法。

6. The report must be finished _____ today.

by
on
at
in

✔ by
解析：by 表示「在…之前」。

7. The train arrived _____ time.

on
in
at
with

✔ on
解析：on time 表示準時。

8. The price has _____ increased.

recently
recent
recents
more recent

✔ recently
解析：副詞修飾動詞。

9. Please contact us if you need more _____.

information
inform
informed
informing

✔ information
解析：need 後面接名詞。

10. He was absent _____ work yesterday.

from
to
at
with

✔ from
解析：be absent from 為固定用法。
